About Us

Smart Attendance Management System

Built for educational institutions to streamline attendance tracking and reporting

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, efficient, and user-friendly attendance management system that helps educational institutions maintain accurate records, identify at-risk students early, and generate insightful reports automatically.

Our Vision

To become the leading attendance management solution in the education sector by leveraging modern technology, data structures, and analytics to improve student engagement and academic success.

Key Features

Role-Based Access Control

Secure authentication system with separate dashboards for Admin, HOD, Faculty, and Students.

Real-Time Tracking

Live attendance marking with instant updates and notifications for low attendance.

Automated Defaulter Detection

Automatic identification of students below 75% attendance threshold with early warning system.

Comprehensive Reports

Daily, weekly, monthly, and subject-wise attendance reports in PDF format.

Timetable Management

Admin-defined weekly schedules with automatic lecture generation based on academic calendar.

Audit Trail System

Complete history of attendance modifications with timestamp and faculty information.

Advanced Search

Optimized student search using Binary Search Tree (BST) for O(log n) performance.

Data Visualization

Matplotlib-powered charts showing attendance trends and subject-wise analytics.
