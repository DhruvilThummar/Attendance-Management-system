About Attendify

Attendify is a modern, cloud-based attendance management system designed specifically for educational institutions.

Our platform helps schools, colleges, and universities digitize their attendance processes, reduce paperwork, and gain valuable insights into student attendance patterns.

  • Multi-tenant architecture
  • Role-based access control
  • Automated report generation
  • Parent portal access
